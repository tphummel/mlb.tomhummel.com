Ruben Sierra's age-27 season with the A's was a rough year at the plate that still produced a 20/20 season and 101 RBI.

<!--more-->

- 158 games, 692 PA, 147 hits, .233/.288/.390, 22 HR, 25 SB, 101 RBI.
- -1.6 WAR, 1.0 oWAR, -3.2 dWAR.
- Crazy that an everyday player put up 101 RBI and a 20/20 season on a .678 OPS.
